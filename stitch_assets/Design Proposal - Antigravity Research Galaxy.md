# Design Direction: Antigravity Research Galaxy

## Visual Identity: Google Antigravity (2026 Aesthetic)
*   **Surface:** Weightless, floating cards with glassmorphism (variable blur).
*   **Palette:** "Dark-Mode Fluorescents" — Obsidian (#0A0A0A) backgrounds with neon accents (Cyan, Magenta, Electric Lime).
*   **Motion:** Elastic, zero-gravity inertia. Elements react to hover with soft-decay "breathing" animations.
*   **Depth:** Multi-layered Z-index hierarchy with dynamic shadowing.

## Proposed Screens

1.  **Knowledge Galaxy (Home):** A generative, 3D-mapped visualization of research papers clustered by semantic relationship. Papers float as weightless nodes in an infinite obsidian void.
2.  **Semantic Focus (Paper Detail):** When a node is selected, it expands into a translucent, floating workspace. Related citations orbit the main paper like satellites.
3.  **Discovery Stream:** A "Living" feed of new papers that morphs its layout based on real-time scrolling and interaction (Zen mode vs. Data-dense mode).
4.  **Immersive Analytics:** A 3D scrollytelling view showing the impact and trends of specific research fields over time, using fluid data visualizations.
